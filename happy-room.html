About Happy Room

Welcome to Happy Room, a game that combines dark humor with experimental gameplay! Are you ready to step into a lab filled with bizarre secrets and a clone dangling from the ceiling? As the lab owner, your mission is to create ingenious traps and gadgets to test your hapless mannequin.

Gameplay

In this strange yet captivating environment, players are tasked with conducting experiments that are as entertaining as they are gruesome. Unlock new devices and discover a plethora of ways to inflict "happiness" on your clone, all while enjoying the character’s humorous interactions with various traps.

Simply use your computer mouse to design and implement traps, testing their effectiveness on your mannequin. The more creative and destructive your traps, the better!

Optimize Your Score

To achieve a high score in Happy Room, clever design is key. Your score translates to cash, allowing you to purchase even more exciting tools and traps. Experiment with different setups and adjust the pace to create continuous chaos, maximizing damage and fun!
