About Gladihoppers

Step into the arena and prepare for battle in Gladihoppers, an action-packed game where you fight as a gladiator across various thrilling arenas! Whether you prefer to embark on a journey in Career Mode, test your skills in Arcade Mode, engage in a Quick Fight, or practice until you’re perfect, Gladihoppers has something for every warrior at heart.

How to play

Choose Your Fighter

In Gladihoppers, you have the option to select from four unique characters, each with their own special abilities:

Trainer: Boosts your skills and enhances your fighting techniques.

Surgeon: Provides healing abilities to keep you in the fight longer.

Priest: Offers buffs that can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Spy: Utilizes stealth and cunning tactics to outsmart opponents.

Earn Fame and Gold

Victory is sweet! Each win not only brings you fame bonuses but also gold that you can use to upgrade your gladiator. Invest in powerful armor, deadly weapons, and enhance your character's attributes like strength, health, charisma, willpower, and looting skills. The more you win, the stronger you become!

Diverse Game Modes

Career Mode: Progress through a series of challenges and rise to glory.

Arcade Mode: Enjoy quick, action-packed matches against AI or friends.

Quick Fight: Jump right into the action for a fast-paced duel.

Practice: Hone your skills and master your combat techniques before facing real opponents.

Intuitive Controls

Mastering Gladihoppers is easy with its straightforward controls:

Move: Use W/A/S/D to navigate the arena.

Attack: Strike your enemies with the arrow keys.

Throw Javelin: Use the ‘E’ key to launch a javelin at your foes.

Switch Stances: Change your fighting style with the spacebar to adapt to different combat situations.
